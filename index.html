FAQ

Common questions about home visit physiotherapy

Do you provide physiotherapy at home in Guwahati?

Yes — Physio Breath sends a therapist to your home anywhere in Guwahati, with all treatment equipment included.

What conditions do you treat?

We treat back pain, neck pain, joint pain, arthritis, knee pain, frozen shoulder, stroke rehabilitation and post-surgery recovery.

How do I book a home visit?

Use the booking form below, call us directly, or message us on WhatsApp — we'll confirm a time within the day.

What should I prepare before the first session?

A comfortable, open space to move in, loose clothing, and any recent scans or doctor's notes if you have them.

Do you treat elderly patients and stroke survivors?

Yes — we regularly work with elderly patients on balance, falls prevention, and stroke rehabilitation at home.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on the condition and severity. Your therapist will give you a realistic estimate after the first home assessment.
